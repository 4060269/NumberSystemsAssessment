Binary Counter

Binary is very important in Computer Science. Everything in a computer boils down to 0s and 1s. With computers, you often see numbers appear regularly when discussing sizes of things - hard disks, SSDs, memory, network traffic etc.

This is all due to the powers of 2.

When you have one bit, the value can be 0 or 1. So there are 2 possibilities - 0 and 1.

When you have two bits, there are 4 possibilities - 00, 01, 10 and 11

With three bits, there are 8 possibilities - 000, 001, 010, 011, 100, 101, 110, 111

and So on.

2number of bits = the number of possible combinations
